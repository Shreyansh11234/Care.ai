Making Healthcare
Accessible to Everyone

Care.ai was born from a simple belief — that everyone deserves quick, intelligent health guidance, no matter where they are.

Why Care.ai?

Millions of people experience symptoms daily but hesitate to visit a doctor — sometimes due to cost, distance, or simply not knowing if it's serious enough. Care.ai bridges that gap by providing an instant, AI-powered preliminary analysis of your symptoms, photos, and medical reports. It's not a replacement for a doctor — it's the first step toward understanding your health better.

How It Works

Powered by Google's Gemini AI, Care.ai analyzes the text you describe, photos of visible conditions like skin rashes or eye infections, and even medical reports like blood tests and X-rays. It cross-references all inputs holistically to suggest potential conditions and tells you whether you should consult a professional — all within seconds.

Your Privacy Matters

Your data is never stored on any server. Every analysis is processed in real-time and discarded immediately after. No accounts, no tracking, no data selling. Your health information stays yours — always.
